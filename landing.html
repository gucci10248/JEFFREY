What BioMCP Does

Gene Lookup

Get gene function, tissue expression, pathways, variants, and linked drugs — all in one call.

Variant Annotation

Annotate variants like BRAF V600E with population frequency, pathogenicity scores, and clinical evidence.

Clinical Trials

Search trials by condition, gene, drug, or mutation. Filter by phase, status, and location.

Drug Intelligence

Check indications, adverse events, drug-drug interactions, and regulatory status across FDA/EMA.

Literature Synthesis

Search PubMed across 5 backends. Auto-enrich with PubTator gene/disease annotations.

Pathway & Enrichment

Map gene sets to KEGG/Reactome pathways. Run g:Profiler enrichment analysis.
